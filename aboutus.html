Food Network Agro Ltd. is providing seafood, fish and soft shell crabs from its own farm at the sundarbans. It is situated near sundarbans in shatkhira district. We collect our products from our own farm and listed farmers. The collected products are processed fresh and frozen immediately to ensure high quality.
Our products are guaranteed, antibiotic-free, 100% frozen fresh and chemical-free. We don't add preservatives or any other additives. Moreover, food net agro products are com pletely non-gmo.




Our vision

Create a strong brand presence in the market and provide the best quality products to local and foreign markets.




Our mission

Only fresh, safe, organic and premium products are available at foodnet such as sundarbans mangrove-fish, softshell crab, live mangrove crabs and other seafood. We have our own farm at the sundarbans and also collect these products from other well reputed farms here to verify quality at every step, while creating a strong network between the farmers and con sumers to save cost and time.

This strong network would also allow the farmers and fishermen to get afar from the syndicate that's currently driving the market, which will ensure the fishermen and farmers of the sundarbans are paid a fair price for their products.
